# Song Library & Difficulty Guide

**Status: TBD.** We're holding off on programming a fixed song list until we see who shows up and can gauge skill levels across the instruments.

The plan: pick songs that fit the players in the room and grow them over time — a beginner and an advanced player can share a song when the parts are chosen well, so we'll match each seat to the person and step difficulty up as the term goes. Repertoire leans worship, with the occasional secular change-up for range and fun.

Songs (with charts, chord sheets, and reference tracks) will be added here and to the site once the roster settles. An earlier draft library of ~45 songs lived here and is preserved in git history if we want to pull from it later.
